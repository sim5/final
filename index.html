Sky War

  • High Concept: A shooter game in the sky.
  • Player Experience Goals: Concentration to shoot the enemy while avoiding the enemies and their bullets.
  • Genre: Shooter.
  • Game play: The player moves with mouse, shoots with left click, when the power gets to 100%, the player can press 'A' to use the super power. Smiley faces from the top double the shooting speed. Smiley faces from the left allow multiple shots. The effects of the smiley faces disappear if you get a shot or hit an enemy. When you collide with the enemy boss, you die instantly.
  • Prototype goal: Shooting, moving, dodging.
  • Features: Super power is available every 5 kills/hits. Smiley faces from the top doubles the shooting speed. Smiley faces from the left allow multiple shots.
  • Setting/Premise: It is World War III and you are the only pilot from your nation who survived in the battle. You are in the middle of the ocean.
  • Story: It is World War III and you are the only pilot from your nation who survived in the battle. You are in the middle of the ocean. You have to defeat the enemy boss to win the battle.
  • Target Audience: Any age group who can control with mouse and keyboard.
  • Play time: Around 2 minutes.
  • Strategies: The player should collect the smiley faces to double the shooting speed and get multiple shots. Also, when the power gets to 100%, the player should press 'A' button to use the super power. Before the boss stage, it is recommended to stay in the lower right corner. In the boss stage, you should avoid colliding with the boss since it kills you instantly if you touch it.
